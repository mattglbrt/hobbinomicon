## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Hobbinomicon. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us at [support@hobbinomicon.com](mailto:support@hobbinomicon.com).

## 2. Who We Are

Our website address is: [https://hobbinomicon.com](https://hobbinomicon.com).

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering on the website, subscribing to our newsletter, expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when participating in activities on the website, or otherwise contacting us.

**Personal Information Provided by You:**
We collect personal information that you provide to us such as name, email address, and contact information.

**Information Collected Automatically:**
We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the website. This information does not reveal your specific identity but may include device and usage information.

**Comments:**
When visitors leave comments on the site, we collect the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.

**Media:**
If you upload images to the website, you should avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS) included.

**Cookies:**
We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. Specific information about how we use such technologies and how you can refuse certain cookies is set out in our Cookie Policy.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes described below. We process your personal information for these purposes in reliance on our legitimate business interests, in order to enter into or perform a contract with you, with your consent, and/or for compliance with our legal obligations.

- To facilitate account creation and logon process.
- To send you marketing and promotional communications.
- To send administrative information to you.
- To analyze user interactions and behavior on our site.
- To protect our services.
- To enforce our terms, conditions, and policies.
- To respond to legal requests and prevent harm.

## 5. Sharing Your Information
