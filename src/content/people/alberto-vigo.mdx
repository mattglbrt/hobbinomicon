Alberto Vigo co-founded Corvus Belli with his brother Carlos in 1998. He has overseen the production and miniatures side of the studio across its entire history, from the original historical wargame lines through every edition of Infinity and into the more recent Aristeia!, Defiance, and Warcrow releases. The look and engineering of the Corvus Belli miniatures range owes much to his direction.
