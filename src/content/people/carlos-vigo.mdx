Carlos Vigo co-founded Corvus Belli with his brother Alberto in 1998. He has been the principal creative voice behind Infinity since the game's first edition in 2005, leading game design, setting development, and the long-running story arcs that drive each new edition. The Human Sphere setting, the ARO system, and the faction structure that defines modern Infinity are all his work alongside the Corvus Belli design team.
