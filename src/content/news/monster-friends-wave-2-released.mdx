## There's also a new 2-player starter kit

Orc the Brand also put out a **[2-player starter kit](https://www.orcthebrand.com/products/two-player-starter-kit)**, and honestly it's the easiest way in for two people. It's a set of monsters picked to actually synergize together, bundled at about a 25% discount, and built to give you a better first game than grabbing models at random.

You get two ready-made parties:

- **The Muckland Dirt Scamps** are a gambling-based, RNG-heavy horde. Lots of little guys, lots of dice, lots of praying it comes up in your favor.
- **The Defenders of The South Coast Plaza** are a buff-based snowball party. They stack their support abilities on top of each other, so once they get going the whole thing is greater than the sum of its parts.

If you've been waiting for a clean on-ramp into Monster Friends, this is it.

## Grab the Mr. Devil before fall

Quick heads up on that Mr. Devil Summer Cook Out. It's a limited seasonal sculpt, and Orc the Brand is planning to stop making it around early fall. So it's a grab-it-now or wait-a-whole-year kind of deal. Like all the alt sculpts it doesn't do anything special in the game, it's just a cool model. That's reason enough for me.

## We're still waiting on the rules

One thing I don't have yet: rules for these new guys. Right now I'm not seeing stats or cards for the Wave 2 monsters, so you can't actually field them at the table just yet. I'll be keeping an eye out, and the second Orc the Brand puts out the rules I'll update this page so you know it's safe to jump in.
