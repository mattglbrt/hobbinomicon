Orc the Brand has released the complete set of **monster rule cards** for [Monster Friends: Battle for New Florida](/games/monster-friends-battle-for-new-florida/). The cards cover stats, abilities, and special rules for every currently playable monster.

The cards are a free download and slot in alongside the core rules already available during the beta.

**[Download the monster rule cards (PDF)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ADY6p2SQdoHxlHPfWByw-eXmxUFXQ3zc/view)**

If you're new to Monster Friends, the [game's directory page](/games/monster-friends-battle-for-new-florida/) has the full setup walkthrough, including the core rulebook, recommended scenario, and where to grab the two-player starter kit.
