If you haven't come across it yet, **Gloam** is a folk-fantasy TTRPG by The Lone Legend that ditches dice entirely in favour of a single deck of tarot cards. Oracle resolution, tactical combat, and even its word-combination magic system all run through the deck, alongside a streamlined lifepath system for character creation.

To celebrate, The Lone Legend ran a **One-Page Adventure Jam**, inviting the community to build one sized Gloam scenarios and share them for free on itch.io. The response was a dozen entries covering everything from fae mischief to haunted crypts. If you own Gloam (or just want to see what people are building with it), every one of these is free to grab:

- **[Dost Thou Know Why the Willow Weeps](https://silvernightingale.itch.io/i-know-why-the-willow-weeps)** by SilverNightingale
- **[Stygian Weald](https://watcherdm.itch.io/stygian-weald)** by WatcherDM
- **[The Ballad of Clangor](https://noble-gnoll.itch.io/the-ballad-of-clangor)** by Noble Gnoll
- **[The Auld Kirk](https://nemysisrelapse.itch.io/the-auld-kirk)** by nemyisrelapse
- **[Beware the Wild Rushes](https://noseatbelts.itch.io/beware-the-wild-rushes)** (WIP) by noseatbelts
- **[A Paladin's Lament](https://ozthearistocrat.itch.io/a-paladins-lament)** by ozthearistocrat
- **[The Plea of Oberon](https://questforquests.itch.io/the-plea-of-oberon)** by Quest for Quests
- **[The Ruined Crypt](https://orestan.itch.io/the-ruined-crypt)** by Orestan
- **[The Lord of Glen-Castle](https://the-lone-legend.itch.io/the-lord-of-glen-castle)** by The Lone Legend
- **[The Long Road](https://oakleyszines.itch.io/the-long-road-solo-gloam-adventure)** by oakley
- **[Come With Me O'er the Mountain](https://onetorch.itch.io/come-with-me-oer-the-mountain)** by OneTorch
- **[Gloaming Wood](https://erathke.itch.io/gloaming-wood)** by E Rathke

Twelve free one-page adventures for a single system is a great haul, and a good chunk of these look solo-friendly at a glance. I'll be picking a few to run through myself. Expect a full write-up here.
