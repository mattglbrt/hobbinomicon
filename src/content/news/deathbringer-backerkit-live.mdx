It's happening. **[Deathbringer](https://www.backerkit.com/c/projects/roll-for-combat/deathbringer-ttrpg-by-professor-dm)**, Professor DM's dark-fantasy TTRPG, has gone live on BackerKit. I didn't hesitate. I backed it the moment I saw the page go up and did not even read the campaign honestly. I am not even sure how many years we have been waiting for this at this point. To be honest I'm actually not that into chainmail bikinis, but I am very interested in the adventures and the ruleset itself.

## Why I'm all in

A few reasons this one hit me square in the chest:

- **I'm a huge Runehammer fan.** Brandon Gillam of RUNEHAMMER and Professor DM are the two main dungeontubers I watch.
- **I've been watching Professor DM for years.** This is the game he's been forging at his own table for decades.
- **I'm newly, deeply in love with Shadowdark.** And here's the kicker: Deathbringer is **fully compatible with Shadowdark and every OSR game**, while still playing by its own rules. Everything I'm building on the Shadowdark side drops straight into this.
- Since Roll for Combat is handling it, this is not a project I'm worried will fizzle in fulfilment.
- It looks like this relies heavily on Ultimate Dungeon terrain which I love, and actually just finished making my own board for.

## And I've got minis on deck

Perfect timing on the hobby side too. I just finished slicing up the **Deathbringer miniatures from Westphalia**. Files are prepped and heading onto the 3D printer later today. So by the time the book's in hand hopefully I will have them all painted.

If you've been on the fence, the campaign is live now and, as I write this, blowing past its goal by the hour. Go [take a look](https://www.backerkit.com/c/projects/roll-for-combat/deathbringer-ttrpg-by-professor-dm).
