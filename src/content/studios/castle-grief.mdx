Castle Grief is the indie tabletop banner behind Kal Arath, a sword-and-sandal OSR fantasy game built for solo, co-op, or GM'd play, and its growing line of supplements (Valley of the Black Ziggurat, Lords of the Pit, Twin Sun Sutra).

Outside Kal Arath, the catalog leans dark fantasy and OSR: hexcrawls (Calen March, Aelwine, Tereghat), Mörk Borg-adjacent adventures (Company of the Fading Star), mass-combat rules (KILLCHAIN), micro-hacks (Knightsblood), and an analog OSR zine (Black March). PDFs live on [itch.io](https://castlegrief.itch.io/), physical zines and supplements ship through the [Shopify store](https://castlegrief.myshopify.com/collections/all), and devlogs land on the [Substack](https://castlegrief.substack.com/).

There's also a Castle Grief Discord, but invites cycle. The current link is posted on the [itch.io page](https://castlegrief.itch.io/).
