Steamforged Games is a UK-based tabletop publisher. They acquired the Iron Kingdoms IP, including Warmachine and Hordes, from Privateer Press in 2023, and currently publish Warmachine MK4. Their wider catalog includes Guild Ball, Epic Encounters, and licensed board games for properties like Dark Souls, Resident Evil, and Monster Hunter.
