Corvus Belli is a Spanish miniatures studio founded in 1998 by brothers Carlos and Alberto Vigo. They started making historical wargame miniatures, then in 2005 released the first edition of Infinity, the sci-fi skirmish game that has become their flagship line and one of the longest-running independent miniatures games in the hobby.

Today the company is based outside Barcelona and is one of the largest privately-held miniatures publishers in Europe. The current Infinity catalog spans eight major factions and dozens of sectorial sub-armies, with both metal and resin/plastic kits, all of it produced in-house. Alongside Infinity they also publish [Aristeia!](https://aristeiathegame.com/) (an arena board game set in the same universe), [Defiance](https://infinitythegame.com/defiance) (a co-operative dungeon-crawl board game), and [Warcrow](https://warcrow.com/) (a fantasy mass-battle game launched in 2024). The Vigo brothers still lead design across the lineup.
