## Lego Tip: Whatnot App

If you like Lego or think about getting into it, I've been exploring the Whatnot app. At first I thought it was a huge scam - definitely wouldn't buy trading cards from it (lots of sketchy stuff there).

But there are tons of retail stores using Whatnot to liquidate inventory. You can get crazy deals on bulk Lego - I just got hundreds of pieces for literally two or three bucks at a time.

Check out Whatnot for actual physical products. Just avoid the cards.
