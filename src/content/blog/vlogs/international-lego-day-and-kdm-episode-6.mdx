## KDM Episode 6: Best Hunt Yet

The hunt phase was incredible:
- Sophia triggered the Bold event, gaining understanding and the Prepared ability
- Got two lion claws during the hunt (finally!)
- Drew Destiny Bound giving us ambush

### The Showdown

Because we ambush, we set up wherever we want. Ground fighting is on, so we need to shoot first.

**Key plays:**
- Death Mask archer crits immediately, knocking the lion down
- Sophia lands fist and tooth wounds for weapon proficiency XP
- Clinestra continues being a crit machine with the scrap sword
- The persistent injury makes him roll to skip turns

**The kill:** Clinestra finishes it with a natural 10 on the wound roll. The monster dies at the end of its turn from a persistent injury.

### Resource Jackpot

- White fur for the lion skin cloak (Sophia can pounce with bone club now)
