## Damage Report

Only one thing broken—clean snaps that I can figure out where they go. This base is cool and hefty—printed solid.

## Supporting Early Adopters

You have to be an early adopter to support the company like this. I believe in what SFG is doing and they're really doing some things great. But for someone new to the hobby, the breakage might be off-putting.

In the scale of things, this is not a huge problem. Still easier to clean up than a Forge World model. I could probably hit up SFG and get a replacement since their customer support is that good, but I'm not going to because that's not cool.
