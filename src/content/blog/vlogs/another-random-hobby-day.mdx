## Board Texturing with Isopropyl Alcohol

We're done with the board for a couple of days now because this stuff needs time. I mixed a bunch of isopropyl alcohol into the texture mix. That's a key step. The isopropyl alcohol gets everything wet so I can put it down how I want it, and then it evaporates out. So you get a liquid mud texture—you're putting down a very soupy mixture, but then the iso evaporates and all the other stuff stays in place.

The areas I applied it to are mostly hard now. You can see it looks like actual dirt. It's a little bit spongy still because it's not fully dry, but it looks like real mud—like a mudslide just came through.
