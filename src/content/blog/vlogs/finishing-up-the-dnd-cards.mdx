## Dark Sun Cards Found

We finally pulled Dark Sun cards from the 1992 AD&D box. The numbering is wild—a Heartwood Spear at card 408 doesn't even appear on my checklist, and Captain Kasil at 662 is labeled Dragonlance. I have no idea what was going on with their numbering system.

I hit a pack where the whole back half was Dark Sun cards, and I was right—the Dark Sun art is way cooler than all the other art. The Ravenloft ones have been great too. Nightmare, Strahd's Skeletal Steed, Quickwood, Assassin Imp—some really cool pulls.

## Highlights from the Last Packs
