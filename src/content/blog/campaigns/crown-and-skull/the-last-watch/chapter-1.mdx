"Icebane has been stolen. The Final Hunt has begun."

Eyes still closed, she takes a breath. For this brief instant, things are still the same, nothing has changed. She exhales, the room turns frigid for a second, so fast its barely perceptible. Her eyes open with a flash of pale blue light before returning to normal. From this moment forward everything is diffirent.

With the wards now removed, her companion removes the loose floorboard and pulls five bundles out of the storage. The floor cries out from the weight of the packs, circular with long slender poles tied to each. The smell of aged oil cloth fills the apartment room to replace the chill moments before.

"I will gather the others."

Now alone she closes her eyes and once again extends her awareness out, through the bustling streets of Rivergate into the surrounding mountainside
