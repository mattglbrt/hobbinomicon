# Warmachine Mark 4 Overview

## Official Links

- [Warmachine App Download Links](https://steamforged.com/pages/iron-kingdoms-warmachine)

## What is Warmachine Mark 4?

### Models
- All models from Mk1 to Mk3 are being phased out of production.
- Models released before August 2022 will no longer be produced after current stock runs out.
- Legacy models will still have rules in Mk4:
  - **Prime Format**: Focused balance updates.
  - **Unlimited Format**: No guaranteed balance updates.

### Rules
- Fully transitioned to a digital format.
- Rules available in the Warmachine App:
  - Core mechanics, model stats, and scenarios.
- Optional subscription ($5/month) unlocks additional features:
  - Extra list storage.
  - Weekly lore and scenario updates.
  - Printing unit stats.

### Unchanged Mechanics
- Dice system (hits, damage, boosting, etc.).
- Focus, Fury, and Essence mechanics (minor tweaks).
- Damage grids and spirals.
- Most movement rules.
- Premeasuring allowed.
- Aiming and engagement rules.

### Major Rule Changes
- **List Building**: Armies determine playable models, not Factions.
- **Magic Stats**: Replaced by ARC, AAT, and CTRL for streamlined magic.
- **Facing**: Models have 360-degree vision; no facing rules.
- **Free Strikes**: Removed; movement out of melee has consequences.

## What Happened to Hordes?
- Hordes has been folded into Warmachine.
- Hordes models are still usable but no longer categorized separately.

## Mark 4 Models
- Modular design for Warjacks and Warbeasts.
- Customizable components like heads, arms, and hardpoints.

## Command Cards and Mercenaries
- **Command Cards**: One-time-use abilities.
- **Mercenaries**: Integrated into Armies as regular units.

## Cadres in Warmachine Mk4
- Cadres are models usable in multiple Armies within a Faction.
- Example: Morayne, The Blade of Twilight, is a Cadre Leader for the Dusk Faction and playable in multiple Dusk Armies.
- Legacy Armies: Many models are usable across different Prime Armies.

For more details, refer to the Warmachine App or official documentation.
