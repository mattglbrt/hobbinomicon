## State of the Game

If you are a returning player or have been out of the loop, a lot has changed for Warmahordes. The game is in its 4th version of rules, previously called Mk4. Now the game is just called Warmachine and there is no Hordes designation.

[If you want to jump right in download the 100% free app and get started rolling dice.](https://steamforged.com/pages/warmachine-app)

The game itself has split into two different formats – Prime and Unlimited. Prime is the current up-to-date competitive format and contains both the new Factions created for mk4 and a selection of mk3 faction army lists, essentially themes from mk3. Unlimited contains rules for all of the mk3 models. Most tournaments and steamrollers are held in the Prime format. Currently all of the new MK4 factions are entirely 3d printed, with no option to buy STLs and print them yourself. Every legacy faction is now out of print, but the community has been assured that the army lists in the Prime format will receive balance updates.

A quick touch on the balance updates – every January the game receives the balance update for the year where rules are updated and nerfs and buffs are handed out.

If you are a new player, coming in Q4 of 2024 a new plastic starter set is coming from Steamforged games.

Privateer Press recently (Q3 of 2024) sold everything Warmachine related to Steamforged Games. Steamforged games is doing their best to improve the manufacturing and distribution pipeline for warmachine.
