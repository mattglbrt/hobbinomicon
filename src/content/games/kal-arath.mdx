## What is Kal Arath

Kal Arath is a 56-page Old School Revival fantasy adventure RPG by [Castle Grief](/studios/castle-grief/). The setting is a savage steppe (also called The Kyrg by northern barbarians) where demon lords demand sacrifice and pit-fighters can become kings. It's basically Conan distilled into hexcrawl shape.

The system uses 2d6 core resolution, classless character progression, player-facing rolls, and exploding damage dice. Combat is fast and extremely deadly. The whole game is one self-contained booklet of rules, setting, and tables, built for solo, co-op, or group play. You can also lift just the setting and procedural gameplay onto any other fantasy TTRPG you prefer.

## How to start

1. **Buy the $5 PDF** at the [Castle Grief itch page](https://castlegrief.itch.io/kal-arath). 56 pages, completely self-contained.
2. **Roll a character.** Classless, 2d6, takes a few minutes.
3. **Drop into the steppe.** The book has the procedural tools to run a session out of the box.

If you fall in love with it, the supplements (Valley of the Black Ziggurat, Lords of the Pit, Twin Sun Sutra) are $5 each on the same itch page. Print copies of the core book and supplements are available through Castle Grief's [Shopify store](https://castlegrief.myshopify.com/collections/all).
