## What is Trench Crusade?

Trench Crusade is a grimdark, alternate-history skirmish wargame set in a world where a Crusader knight opened a gate to Hell 700 years ago. The setting has been locked in apocalyptic trench warfare ever since, blending WWI-era technology with holy war, heresy, and body horror. It's designed by Mike Franchina and Tuomas Pirinen (of Mordheim fame).

The game grew out of kitbash and conversion culture, and it leans into that hard: the rules are free to download, and the community has always been encouraged to build and proxy their own warbands rather than buy a fixed range.

## How to start

1. **Grab the free rules** from the [official site](https://www.trenchcrusade.com/).
2. **Two players and a small skirmish-sized table.**
3. **A warband of models.** This is where the kitbashing comes in. Plenty of players build their Trench Pilgrims and Heretic Legion from bits, though official miniatures exist too.
