## What is Relicblade

Relicblade is an indie tactics-RPG miniatures game designed and produced by Sean Sutter at Metal King Studio. Players build a party of 4 to 6 heroic characters and play out short, focused tactical games on a 2'×2' board. Sessions run around 30 minutes, so it's easy to fit multiple games into an evening.

Parties are built by alignment rather than by faction. Pick good or evil, then recruit any combination of characters and upgrades from that side. A dynamic action dice system gives each character meaningful turn-to-turn decisions, and the rules stay light enough that you can focus on tactics instead of looking things up.

The game is not miniature-agnostic. Relicblade ships its own line of detailed metal miniatures, along with lavishly illustrated character and upgrade cards that are part of the play experience.

## How to start

1. **Read the free rules** at the [Learn to Play page](https://www.relicblade.com/learn-to-play).
2. **Pick a party** of 4 to 6 characters from the Metal King Studio catalog and grab the matching minis and cards.
3. **Set up a 2'×2' play area** with terrain.
